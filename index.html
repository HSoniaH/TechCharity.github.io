What is TECHARITY

TECHARITY is an online event, organised for its first edition by the scientific club of ESI (CSE) . During [4days ?] , paticipants will form teams, each team will create its own website, this last will be evaluated by how many views it will get, then the most viewed one will be the winner ...

How can I help by participating ?

In fact, each view is considered as a donation, ( 1 view = …DZA ?) , that’s to say ;you can contribute in the event and help ; only by sharing the websites so that it will reach the maximum of views hence the maximum of donations .

To whom the money will be given ?

SOLIMED and LE CŒUR SUR LA MAIN are the charitable organisations that we are collaborating with, they are recognised as a public utility whose statues and actions are inspired by altruism and the public interest, so they will take charge of giving the donations to the right people.

What will i benefit ?

There’s a worshop that will take place at the first day, it will deal with the basics of the conception of a website .
